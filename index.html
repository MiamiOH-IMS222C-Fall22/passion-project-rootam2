What is Fast Fashion

Cheap, trendy clothing that samples ideas from the catwalk or celebrity culture and turns them into garments in high street stores at breakneck speed to meet consumer demand. Many big brands do this regardless of the impact on the environment and people's health.

Ways to Donate

Donating clothes is the best way to prevent fast fashion. This trend begain to rise up in late 2018. Still popular today, many people donate their clothes to thrift stores or host clothing swaps. Here are seven ways to prevent fast fashion:

  • Shop from sustainable and ethical fashion brands
  • Buy less often and buy high quality
  • Donate or sell gently used clothing
  • Host a clothing swap
  • Buy or rent secondhand clothes
  • Recycle textiles and garments
  • Repurpose old clothing
